ABOUT US

The unique blend of quality and affordability is not easy to encounter and experience in the educational arena. Very few institutions can proudly flaunt this tag with elan and among them, Jeppiaar college of arts and science stands tall imparting excellence in Arts and expertise in Science. The college is the visionary dream of Col. Dr.Jeppiaar who envisioned an enriching education that will equip the students with life skills, enhance their innate capabilities and empower them to adorn the zenith of their careers. The sprawling campus is located in a salubrious ambiance away from the hustle and bustle of the metropolitan city.

VISION

Our institution will provide excellent opportunities for the highest level of cognitive learning to the student community to master over the social and economical challenges and give the best solution to the national and global communities.
